Questions

Answers to the most common ones.

A voucher's active period depends on the package you choose. For example, a daily package is active for 24 hours from your first login, a weekly one for 7 days, and so on. The exact duration is shown on each package card.

Each voucher can only be used on one device at a time. To switch devices, log out from the active one first, then log in on the new device.

Note down or screenshot your voucher right after buying! But if you forget, chat us on WhatsApp with your Order ID or the phone number used to pay, and we'll help you find it.

We accept QRIS payments — scan with any e-wallet or banking app (GoPay, OVO, DANA, ShopeePay, m-banking, etc.).
